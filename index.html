— Our belief

The future of AI isn’t the model. It’s the moment someone trusts it.

Most AI products get built backwards — technology first, user experience as an afterthought. Antiphono starts where it should: with real people, real needs, and the research to understand both.

We exist at the intersection of rigorous research and interaction design. The result is AI that people actually adopt — not because they have to, but because it feels built for them.

trust
How we work Three-stage process
  • 01

    Research

    We spend time with real users before a pixel is drawn. Understanding behavior, mental models, and unmet needs — not assumptions about what AI should do. Every engagement begins with listening.

  • 02

    Design

    We build interaction patterns native to AI — honest about uncertainty, clear about capability, intuitive at first touch. Interfaces that reveal power without exposing complexity.

  • 03

    Validate

    We test continuously with real users in real scenarios. Not wireframes — working prototypes with measurable outcomes. We ship when trust is earned, not just when time is up.
