02 / Philosophy

How we think

01

Trust is designed,
not assumed.

Every confidence score, citation and fallback is an interface decision before it's a model decision.

02

Research before
conviction.

We test assumptions against real users before they calcify into roadmap commitments.

03

Uncertainty is a
first-class state.

Probabilistic systems need interfaces that can say "maybe" clearly, not just "yes" or "error."

04

Ship small,
measure always.

We prefer narrow releases with instrumented feedback loops over sweeping, unmeasured launches.
